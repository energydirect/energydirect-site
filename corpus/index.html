How electricity choice works in Corpus Christi

In Corpus Christi, AEP Texas maintains the poles and wires, while retail electricity providers like Ambit offer the plans and customer support. You can compare options for your exact address, then enroll online with a standard move-in or a self-selected start date.

ZIPs across the Bay Area, Calallen, Flour Bluff, and Padre Island can see different offers, which is why checking availability by ZIP is essential. If your household usage tends to exceed 1000 kWh during summer, look closely at any bill credit thresholds or tiered pricing listed in each plan’s EFL.

Tip: Use the EFL to compare average prices at 500 / 1000 / 2000 kWh, and check for base charges, minimum usage fees, and early termination fees.
