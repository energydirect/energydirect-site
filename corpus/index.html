How electricity choice works in Corpus Christi

Corpus Christi is in AEP Texas Central’s delivery territory, which means the utility maintains poles and wires, while you can choose a retail electricity provider for your plan. Whether you live in Calallen, Flour Bluff, or Padre Island, plan availability can vary by ZIP code.

High humidity and long cooling seasons in the Coastal Bend mean many households use more electricity in summer months. Always review the EFL to see pricing at 500 / 1000 / 2000 kWh and check for bill credits or minimum usage fees that may apply to your usage pattern.

Corpus Christi FAQs

Who handles outages?
AEP Texas manages delivery and outage restoration, regardless of which retail provider you choose. Visit AEP Texas outage center or check the AEP outage map.

Are time-of-use plans available?
Some providers offer plans with on-peak/off-peak pricing. Check the EFL to see if it matches your lifestyle.

Will switching interrupt my power?
No. Switching providers is seamless, and your lights stay on during the change. For support, see AEP Texas customer support.
