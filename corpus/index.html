How electricity choice works in Corpus Christi

AEP Texas maintains delivery (poles and wires) while retail electricity providers offer plans and billing. Neighborhoods like Calallen, Flour Bluff, and Padre Island can see different offers by ZIP, which is why it’s important to compare for your address and read the EFL.

Corpus Christi FAQs

Who handles outages?
AEP Texas, regardless of your provider. Use the Outage Center or Outage Map.

Do time-of-use plans exist?
Some providers offer on-peak/off-peak plans. Check the EFL to see if the schedule fits your routine.

Does switching interrupt power?
No—service stays on during provider changes. For TDU help, visit AEP Texas support.
