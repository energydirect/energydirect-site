How Solar Buyback Works in Texas

In competitive retail areas, certain electricity plans credit you for excess solar your system exports to the grid. Credit methods and caps vary by provider and TDU service territory.

1

Produce & Use

Your rooftop PV powers your home first. A smart meter records what you use and what you export.

2

Export Excess

When production exceeds usage, excess flows to the grid through your TDU (Oncor, CenterPoint, AEP Texas, TNMP).

3

Receive Credits

Eligible plans provide bill credits. The credit type (1:1, rate-based, or capped) is specified in the plan‚Äôs EFL.
