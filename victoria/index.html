How electricity choice works in Victoria

AEP Texas maintains electric delivery; retail providers offer the plans and billing. Offers can vary by ZIP and usage, so compare for your specific address and read each plan’s EFL.

Summer heat and humidity can increase kWh. Review EFL averages at 500 / 1000 / 2000 kWh and note any base charges, minimum usage fees, or bill credits that may affect your bill.
