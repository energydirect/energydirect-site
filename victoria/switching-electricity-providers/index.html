What to Know Before You Switch Electricity Providers in Victoria, TX

Switching providers starts with reviewing plan documents and your current contract dates. Here’s a quick checklist to keep it smooth.

1) Review Your Current Contract

  • Check the end date and any early termination fees.
  • Look at your current plan’s EFL for terms you liked or didn’t.

2) Compare New Plans

  • Open the EFL for each plan you’re considering.
  • Compare contract length, energy charges, delivery charges, and any fees.

3) Watch the Timeline

Switching timing varies by plan and TDU processes. You’ll see the expected start date during enrollment and on your confirmation email.

4) Submit Enrollment

Have your service address and identification details ready. Confirm any deposit requirements if prompted during checkout.

After You Switch

  • Save the EFL, Terms of Service, and confirmation for your records.
  • Check your final bill with the previous provider.
  • For outages, contact your local TDU (number shown on your bill).
