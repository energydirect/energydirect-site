Who handles your billing after you enroll

A quick note so you know exactly who to contact for what.

Ambit Energy handles your account

Once you are enrolled, Ambit Energy handles all billing and account service: your monthly bill, payments, AutoPay, paperless settings, and your MyAmbit online profile. Use the payment methods below or sign in at my.ambitenergy.com.

Energy Direct is your local point of contact

Energy Direct is your local independent Ambit consultant. We help you compare, choose, and switch plans by ZIP. We do not process your payments, but if you need help deciding on a plan or switching, call or text (361) 582-9724.

Ways to pay your Ambit bill

Pay online (MyAmbit)

  • Sign in at my.ambitenergy.com.
  • View and download your bills.
  • Turn on paperless billing.
  • Manage your profile and make a one-time payment.

AutoPay

  • Set automatic payments from a checking or savings account, or a credit or debit card.
  • Never miss a due date.
  • Pair AutoPay with paperless billing to earn an "ePlan" rate discount on eligible plans.

Pay by phone

  • Call (877) 282-6248.
  • Live agents available Monday through Sunday, about 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. CT.

Pay by mail

  • Mail your payment to:

Ambit Energy
P.O. Box 660462
Dallas, TX 75266-0462

Pay with cash

  • Cash payments are accepted at partner retail locations.
  • Sign in to MyAmbit to find participating cash-payment partners near you.

Accepted payment types

  • Visa, Mastercard, and Discover.
  • Bank draft from a checking or savings account.
  • Cash at participating retail partners.

Ambit payment questions

How do I pay my Ambit Energy bill online?

Sign in to your MyAmbit account at my.ambitenergy.com. From there you can view your bill, make a one-time payment, turn on paperless billing, and manage your profile.

How do I pay my Ambit bill by phone?

Call (877) 282-6248. Live agents are available Monday through Sunday, roughly 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. Central Time.

What is the AutoPay and paperless ePlan discount?

When you pair AutoPay with paperless billing, you can earn an "ePlan" rate discount on eligible plans. This is a current Ambit promotion, conditions apply — confirm current terms at enrollment or in your MyAmbit account.

Where do I mail an Ambit payment?

Mail your payment to Ambit Energy, P.O. Box 660462, Dallas, TX 75266-0462. Cash payments are also accepted at participating retail partner locations.

Does Energy Direct handle my payments?

No. Once you enroll, Ambit Energy handles all billing and account service. Energy Direct is your local independent Ambit consultant and your point of contact for comparing, choosing, and switching plans. Call or text us at (361) 582-9724 for help with your plan.
