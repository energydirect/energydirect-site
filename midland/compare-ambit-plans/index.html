Availability in Midland and the Oncor Service Area

Midland residents are served by the Oncor Transmission and Distribution Utility (TDU), which delivers electricity to homes and businesses. Ambit Energy provides retail service in deregulated areas of the Oncor region, including Midland and nearby Odessa.

To confirm plan availability, use the ZIP lookup on the enrollment page. Rates vary by ZIP and usage, so reviewing your Electricity Facts Label (EFL) before enrolling is essential.

Understanding Plan Types

Fixed-rate plans provide rate stability throughout the contract term. Variable-rate plans can fluctuate monthly based on market conditions. The EFL for each plan lists energy charges, TDU delivery fees, and contract terms.

Steps to Compare and Enroll

  1. Visit the Ambit Energy plan page and enter your ZIP code.
  2. Open the EFL and Terms of Service documents.
  3. Compare plan features, term lengths, and fees.
  4. Complete enrollment securely through EnergyDirect.org.

TDU Delivery Charges in Midland

Delivery charges are regulated by Oncor and apply to all providers equally. These fees cover transmission, maintenance, and meter services. Learn more on our TDU Delivery Charges Explained page.

EnergyDirect assists customers in Midland, Odessa, and surrounding Oncor TDU areas. Residents across West Texas can explore Ambit Energy options and review plan details before signing up.
