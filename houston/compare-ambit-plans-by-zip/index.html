Compare Ambit Energy Plans in Houston by ZIP

This quick guide shows how to check availability by ZIP, review plan documents, and enroll online—without making any savings claims.

Quick Steps to Compare

  1. Go to the official plan page and enter your ZIP.
  2. Open the Electricity Facts Label (EFL) for each plan you’re considering.
  3. Compare contract terms, fees, and plan type (fixed or variable).
  4. Choose the plan that fits your household’s needs and enroll online.
Check Rates & Enroll (Houston)

Eligibility by ZIP

Many Houston-area ZIP codes are in deregulated service areas. Availability can vary by address, so the ZIP lookup on the enrollment page is the best way to confirm what’s offered in your neighborhood.

Fixed vs. Variable Plans

Fixed-rate plans typically keep the energy charge constant during the contract term. Variable plans can change month to month. Review each plan’s EFL for details and current terms.

Contract Term & EFL Basics

The EFL summarizes key details: term length, energy charges, delivery charges, fees, and disclosures. Compare EFLs side by side before you enroll.

Fees to Review

  • TDU delivery charges: Transmission and distribution charges from the local utility.
  • Early termination fee: If applicable, listed in the EFL.
  • Other fees: Any additional charges and conditions are explained in the EFL.

How to Enroll Online

  1. Use the ZIP tool to see available plans.
  2. Open and read the plan documents (EFL, Terms of Service, Your Rights as a Customer).
  3. Complete the online enrollment with accurate service address details.
