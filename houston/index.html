FAQ

How do I switch electricity providers in Houston?

Simply choose a plan and sign up online. Ambit coordinates the switch with your utility, and your power stays on.

Does Ambit require a deposit?

Some plans may require a deposit depending on eligibility. You’ll see this during the enrollment process.

Can I take my plan if I move?

If you move within the CenterPoint service area, Ambit can transfer your plan. Otherwise, you can enroll in a new plan at your new address.
