How electricity choice works in Lubbock

Lubbock is in the process of moving into Texas’ deregulated market. LP&L maintains poles and wires, while retail electricity providers like Ambit will handle billing and plans.

As deregulation expands, households in Wolfforth, Slaton, and surrounding areas can shop for plans while LP&L continues delivery service.

No. LP&L continues to maintain lines and delivery, even if you choose a different retail provider.
