How electricity choice works in Lubbock

Lubbock participates in the competitive market. The local delivery utility maintains lines and meters, while retail providers offer plans and billing. Compare plans for your specific address and usage patterns.

Seasonal swings (heating and summer cooling) can change monthly usage. Check EFL averages at 500 / 1000 / 2000 kWh, and watch for base charges, minimum usage fees, or bill credits.

Is Lubbock part of the Texas competitive market?
Yes. Lubbock joined the competitive ERCOT market, giving residents the ability to choose retail electricity providers.

Contact Lubbock Power & Light (LP&L) for delivery-related issues and outages.

Yes. Availability can differ by ZIP. Always check your address to see what plans apply.
