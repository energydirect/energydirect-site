Moving Electric Service in Lubbock

If you’re moving within Lubbock or relocating to the city, Ambit Energy makes it simple to transfer or start your service. Use this quick guide to avoid interruptions during your move.

Steps to Start or Transfer Service

  1. Provide your new service address in Lubbock during enrollment.
  2. Select your preferred Ambit Energy plan.
  3. Schedule your service start date for the day you move in.
  4. Confirm details and submit enrollment online.
