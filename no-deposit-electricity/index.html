How no-deposit switching works

The no-deposit question, answered honestly

  • You may qualify for no deposit: Ambit runs a quick credit check, and many customers start service with no deposit at all.
  • If a deposit does apply: Ambit offers flexible and split-deposit options so you are not asked for the full amount up front.
  • Same-day start: qualifying customers can begin service the same day.

Ambit does not promise zero deposit for everyone. Whether you owe a deposit depends on the credit check.

Getting your cancellation fee back

  • Switch to a featured fixed-rate plan with a 12-month or longer term.
  • Send your final bill showing the early-termination fee to correction@ambitenergy.com.
  • Get a bill credit of up to $250, applied within about two billing cycles.

This is a current Ambit promotion; conditions apply, and you should confirm current terms at enrollment. Customers switching from TXU, Express, 4Change, Veterans, and Tri-Eagle are not eligible for the reimbursement.

No-deposit and switching questions

Can I really get electricity with no deposit?

You may qualify for service with no deposit, based on a quick credit check. Many Ambit customers start with no deposit at all. If a deposit does apply, Ambit offers flexible and split-deposit options so you are not asked for the full amount up front, with same-day start available. Ambit does not guarantee a zero deposit for everyone.

How does the cancellation-fee reimbursement work?

It is a current Ambit promotion. When you switch to a featured fixed-rate Ambit plan with a 12-month or longer term, Ambit will reimburse your previous provider's early-termination or cancellation fee as a bill credit of up to $250. To claim it, send your final bill showing the early-termination fee to correction@ambitenergy.com, and the credit is applied within about two billing cycles. Conditions apply, and you should confirm current terms at enrollment.

Is anyone not eligible for the fee reimbursement?

Yes. Customers switching from TXU, Express, 4Change, Veterans, and Tri-Eagle are not eligible for the cancellation-fee reimbursement. The promotion also requires switching to a featured fixed-rate plan with a 12-month or longer term. Confirm current terms at enrollment.

Will switching interrupt my power?

No. Switching never interrupts your power. Your Transmission and Distribution Utility keeps delivering electricity to your home exactly as before, and Ambit handles the switch behind the scenes. There is no need for anyone to visit your home and no gap in service.

Is there any cost to compare or to get help?

No. Comparing plans and getting help from Energy Direct is free. You enroll directly in the plan you choose.
