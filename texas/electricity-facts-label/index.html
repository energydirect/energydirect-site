Electricity Facts Label (EFL) Explained

Every Texas electricity plan comes with an Electricity Facts Label (EFL). This standardized document helps you compare plans by showing key details in a clear format.

What’s Inside an EFL?

  • Average price per kWh at different usage levels (500, 1000, 2000 kWh).
  • Contract length (typically 12, 24, or 36 months).
  • Base charges and any monthly service fees.
  • Renewable content percentage of the electricity mix.
  • Early termination fees, if applicable.

Why the EFL Matters

Because all retail electricity providers in Texas must use the same format, the EFL makes it easier for customers to compare different plans side by side before enrolling.
