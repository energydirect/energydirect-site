FAQs

Are delivery charges included in the energy rate?

No. Energy charges and TDU delivery charges are listed separately. Review both before enrolling.

Can plan prices change?

Variable plans can change month-to-month. Fixed plans aim to keep the energy charge stable for the term. Always confirm in the EFL.

How do I compare plans fairly?

Open each EFL side-by-side. Compare plan type, term length, energy charges, TDU charges, and fees under the same usage level.
