Common questions about where we serve

Do you only serve the Coastal Bend?

No. We are based in the Coastal Bend of South Texas, but because Ambit enrollment is done online, we help homeowners across the deregulated Texas market. Your eligibility depends on your home's address, not on where we are based.

How do I know if my address is covered?

Enter your ZIP code in the form on this page. The tool reads your market and delivery utility and shows the exact plans available at your address. Most deregulated Texas addresses are covered.

What is a TDU, and why does it matter?

Your Transmission and Distribution Utility, such as CenterPoint, Oncor, AEP Texas, TNMP, or Lubbock Power and Light, owns the poles and wires and delivers your power. You choose your retail provider; the TDU still handles delivery and any outages no matter which plan you pick.

My city is not on your list. Can you still help?

Very likely, yes. The cities shown are examples, not the full map. Enter your ZIP and the tool will confirm whether plans are available at your specific address.
