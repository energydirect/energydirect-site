How Texas Choice Works

  • Your TDU (Oncor, CenterPoint, AEP Texas, TNMP) maintains lines/meters.
  • You choose a retail provider and plan features.
  • Delivery charges are set by your TDU and apply to any provider.

FAQ

Will my power go out when I switch?

No. Your lights stay on during provider changes because your local TDU continues delivery.

Who do I contact for an outage?

Outages are handled by your TDU (Oncor, CenterPoint, AEP Texas, or TNMP), regardless of the retail provider you choose.

How do I compare plans for my address?

Select your ZIP on the plans page to see options. Rates vary by ZIP and usage—always review the EFL.
