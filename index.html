Frequently Asked Questions

Will my power go out when I switch?

No. Your lights stay on during provider changes because your local TDU continues delivery.

Who do I contact for an outage?

Outages are handled by your TDU (Oncor, CenterPoint, AEP Texas, or TNMP), regardless of the retail provider you choose.

How do I compare plans for my address?

Select your ZIP on the plans page to see options. Rates vary by ZIP and usage — always review the EFL.

Is there a cost to switch providers?

There is no fee to switch from one retail provider to another. Your local TDU handles the logistics seamlessly.

What makes Ambit Energy different?

Ambit Energy offers flexible plan options, including renewable energy and the Free Energy rewards program for qualified referrals.

How does the Free Energy program work?

You earn Free Energy credits by referring qualified customers to Ambit. Once you reach 15+ referrals, credits are applied to your bill (energy costs only).

Are there solar options available?

Yes! Ambit offers solar buyback plans in many Texas areas. You can generate solar energy and sell excess power back to the grid.

Can I cancel my plan early?

Each plan has different terms. Review your contract for early termination details. Some plans have no cancellation fees.

What is an EFL?

The Electricity Facts Label is a standardized document that shows pricing, fees, contract terms, and renewable content for any energy plan.
