Start Electricity Service in Dallas with Ambit — Step-by-Step

New to Dallas or switching addresses? Here’s a simple checklist to start or transfer electricity service—focused on terms and documents, not savings claims.

Where Service Is Deregulated

Many addresses in the Dallas area fall within deregulated service territories. Use the enrollment ZIP lookup to confirm availability and view plan options for your specific address.

Start / Stop / Transfer: What’s the Difference?

  • Start (Move-In): New service at a new address.
  • Transfer: Move your current plan to your new Dallas address when eligible.
  • Stop: End service at an address you’re leaving.

Enrollment Checklist

  1. Enter your ZIP and select a plan.
  2. Open the plan documents (EFL, Terms of Service, YRAC).
  3. Provide service address, identity details, and preferred start date.
  4. Confirm any deposit requirements and submit.

Timing: Same-Day vs. Next-Day

Timeline depends on the chosen plan and local utility processes. Your enrollment confirmation will show the expected start date.

After You Enroll

  • Keep your confirmation email for reference.
  • Save copies of the EFL and Terms for your records.
  • If you’re switching from another provider, check your prior contract’s end date and any early termination provisions.
