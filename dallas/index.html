Who handles power outages in Dallas?
Oncor Electric Delivery manages outage restoration and line maintenance, no matter which retail electricity provider you choose.

Are free nights or weekends plans available in Dallas?
Yes, some providers offer time-of-use plans in Oncor territory. Always review the EFL to ensure the pricing works for your lifestyle.

Will switching providers interrupt my electricity service?
No. Your power stays on during the switch, since Oncor still delivers your electricity.
